Class MsgExchangeRateVote

Every validator in the validating set (top 100 in total bonded Luna) is required to submit a vote for the current exchange rate of LUNA for every active denomination once per vote period.

Votes are registered through submitting a MsgExchangeRateVote message, which must correspond to a MsgExchangeRatePrevote submitted in the previous vote period.
